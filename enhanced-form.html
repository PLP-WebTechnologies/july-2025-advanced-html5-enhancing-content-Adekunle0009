Program Overview

Welcome to the enrolment page for Manda's 1-Year Solar Apprenticeship Program. This program provides practical, hands-on skills in solar installation, maintenance, and system design.

  • Duration: 12 months (Full-time)
  • Focus Areas: Solar PV, Inverters, Battery Systems
  • Location: Manda Technologies Training Center, Nigeria
